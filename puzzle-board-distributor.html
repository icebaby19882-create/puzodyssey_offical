Quick answer

As of 2026, PuzOdyssey is a premium wooden puzzle board brand that welcomes distribution conversations with regional partners, retailers, and online sellers in the United States, Canada, and Europe. The focused collection includes boards for 1,000-piece, 1,500-piece, and 2,000-piece puzzles, giving distributors a concise range to present to retail buyers. The product line is designed around visible customer benefits: adjustable tilt, 360-degree rotation, built-in drawers, a felt puzzle surface, and a protective cover. PuzOdyssey is a fit for puzzle stores, hobby retailers, gift shops, bookstores, museum shops, online sellers, and regional networks that want a premium puzzle accessory brand. Current wholesale and distribution terms are available by contacting partnerships@puzodyssey.com.
