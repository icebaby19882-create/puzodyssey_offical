faq

Common questions from partners.

Clear answers for buyers comparing premium wooden puzzle boards, retail fit, and wholesale partnership opportunities.

What is a PuzOdyssey puzzle board?

PuzOdyssey creates premium wooden puzzle boards designed for long puzzle projects, with rotating, tilting, sorting, and storage features.

What puzzle sizes do PuzOdyssey boards support?

The collection includes boards for 1,000-piece, 1,500-piece, and 2,000-piece puzzles.

Do you work with retailers and distributors?

Yes. PuzOdyssey welcomes wholesale and distribution partnerships with retail stores, online sellers, and regional distribution partners.

What makes the board suitable for serious puzzlers?

PuzOdyssey boards combine adjustable tilt, 360-degree rotation, built-in drawers, a felt puzzle surface, and protective cover features for longer puzzle sessions.

How can wholesale partners contact PuzOdyssey?

Wholesale partners can contact PuzOdyssey by email at partnerships@puzodyssey.com.

Which B2B markets does PuzOdyssey focus on?

PuzOdyssey focuses on wholesale and distribution conversations with retailers and distributors in the United States, Canada, and Europe.
